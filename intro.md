# BSSDH 2026 — Workshop Materials

Welcome to the workshop materials for **BSSDH 2026** (Baltic Summer School of Digital Humanities).

This site supports two hands-on workshops on bibliographic metadata from the Latvian art criticism dataset *Mākslu kritika*:

1. **Data cleaning with OpenRefine** — Tuesday morning
2. **Analysis and visualisation with Orange Data Mining** — Tuesday afternoon and Wednesday morning

## How to use this site

- Follow the step-by-step guides in the workshop chapters.
- Download data files and Orange workflows from the [Downloads](downloads.md) page.
- Screenshots in the guides illustrate each step.

## Dataset

The workshop uses a cleaned subset of bibliographic metadata from the dataset *Mākslu kritikas datu kopa*

It is a DH-friendly metadata corpus documenting Latvian art criticism from 2015 to 2025 in newspapers, journals, and online portals.

The records include reviews of theatre performances, books, exhibitions, concerts, films, and related cultural coverage.

The main file is `metadata_bssdh.csv` (~57,900 records). Supporting files include UDC labels, geodata, and Latvian stopwords for text mining.
